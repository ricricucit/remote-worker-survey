What & Why

Compiled by the team behind the Digital Nomad Survey which garnered hundreds of responses from all over the world, the Remote Work Survey aims to collect extensive data on the wider global movement of remote working - and share the results with you. Our dream is to be at the forefront of knowledge about the future of work, and we need your help.

If you’re a remote employee or location independent professional, we want to know about your experiences, trials and tribulations, what turns you on and what grinds your gears. We want to get to the bottom of what makes the remote movement tick, and better understand how it's transformed the 21st century workplace.
